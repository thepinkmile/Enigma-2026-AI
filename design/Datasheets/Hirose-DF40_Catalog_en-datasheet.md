### Polarity and keying

- The DF40 connector body has **no polarity** (Note 4 on all catalog pages).
  The mating engagement is identical in both orientations.
- Keying is enforced by an **asymmetric mounting hole pattern** on the AM board:
  - MH1/MH3 (left pair): 7mm from left edge, 7mm from top/bottom
  - MH2/MH4 (right pair): 7mm from right edge, 12mm from top/bottom
  - 180° rotation produces non-matching positions - see AM Design_Spec.md DR-AM-03

### Handling notes (pages 21-23)

- Self-alignment range: ±0.33 mm in single X and Y direction.
- Mate **parallel** - align ribs first, drop straight in; do not rock or apply excessive force.
- Unmate from **pitch direction** if parallel extraction is not possible; never unmate from **width direction**.
- Do not support PCB weight on connector alone - use screws, posts, or standoffs.
- Because board-to-board connectors can disengage on impact, the mounting hole + standoff arrangement also serves as mechanical retention.
