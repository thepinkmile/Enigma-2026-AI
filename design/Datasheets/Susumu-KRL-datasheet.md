Dimension key (refer to component drawing on PDF page 2):

- **W** = body length along the long axis (along the pad row); for KRL6432T4: 6.40 mm
- **L** = body length along the short axis (across the pad row); for KRL6432T4: 3.20 mm
- **a** = outer terminal (voltage-sense pad) length along W
- **b** = inner terminal (current pad) length along W
- **c** = gap between adjacent pads
- **d** = span from outer edge of first inner pad to outer edge of second inner pad
- **t** = component height above PCB

### Circuit Diagram — 4-Terminal Kelvin Layout

Terminals are arranged along the W axis as: **V — I — (resistive element) — I — V**

- **V (outer pads):** Voltage sense terminals — low-current, high-impedance Kelvin sense path
- **I (inner pads):** Current path terminals — carry the high current through the resistive element

This arrangement separates the sense voltage from IR drops at the current terminal contact
resistance, enabling accurate four-wire (Kelvin) resistance measurement.

### Derating Curve

Power is derated linearly with terminal temperature across the full operating range
−55°C to +155°C (Code M). Refer to the derating curve graph on PDF page 2 for exact values.
X-axis: terminal temperature (°C); Y-axis: ratio to rated power (%).
